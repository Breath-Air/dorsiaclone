We're here to uplift an industry.

Dorsia is a members-only restaurant booking platform serving New York City, Miami, Los Angeles, and The Hamptons; and we're rapidly expanding. Unlike existing dining reservation systems, Dorsia leverages a unique supply-and-demand pricing model to offer hard-to-book reservations for a guaranteed minimum spend.
‍
Our founding executive team and investors bring a wide range of deep expertise across the hospitality, technology, finance, and media industries, providing Dorsia with a unique edge in building a new kind of restaurant tech brand.

Vision

Revolutionize the reservation experience.

Mission

We're on a mission to build frictionless technology that optimizes revenue for restaurants and experiences for members.

Strategy

Dorsia’s revenue optimization model solves supply-and-demand inefficiencies in the restaurant industry by mutually guaranteeing tables for diners and revenue for restaurants. We're creating a curated marketplace where it hasn’t existed before, powering the most coveted restaurants of our time.
